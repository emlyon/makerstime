Explore mobility. 
What do you know about mobility?

This individual research exercise will give you a better understanding of the context around mobility: what is it ? how is it changing ? what are the different applications in the tech industry ? what is its future ? what is its impact on the society ? etc.

To this end, you will make deep researches about robotic in at least 5 of the following disciplinary fields:
  • sociology (explore robotic through sociology)
  • art / design
  • cinematography
  • literature
  • anthropology
  • history
  • geography
  • biology
  • linguistics / etymology
  • philosophy
  • psychology


Deliverables

     01.  A visual restitution of your researches,
     02.  A bibliography of sources (internet, books, exhibition, etc) 

Deliverables have to be sent using the dropbox in your assignments sub-module before February 16 midnight.
- Your document must be a pdf.
- Your document must be named like this : ECU_YOURNAME.pdf or STE_YOURNAME.pdf or PAR_YOURNAME.pdf depending on your campus.
