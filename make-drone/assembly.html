Assembly of your drone

access_time 3 hours

Customization is mandatory
Before getting started with your assembly. We remind you that customization is part of the evaluation, you need to customized your drone. This is an example of a customized bumpers: Be creative and make your drone different!
1. Materials

Make sure you have 3D printed all the components needed.

2. Bumpers assembly

Plug the bumpers into the chassis. Flat side upward !! The white bumpers represent the front of the drone, the red bumpers the back.

3. Arms assembly

Plug the motor arms into the chassis. Yes, you need to press hard.

4. Motors assembly

Place the motors into the arms holes.

Start placing the motors with blue and red wires. Make sure your chassis is positionned exactly like the picture.
Then place the motors with black and white wires.
