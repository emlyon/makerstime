make your drone

In this part, you will learn how to make a mini drone and to customize it!
Keep in mind that your drone must be working before the end of explorations time.
So take care of it during all this part! electronics & 3d parts you will manipulate can be fragile.
Enjoy this time, this is pure learning by doing!

To make & customize your drone, respect carefully the following steps:
  • 1. unbox!
    That means that you have already gotten your kit at makers' lab
  • 2. design & 3d print propellers protections
    Use 2d & 3d design to make it! Don't forget to customize yours.
  • 3. assemble your drone
    Use your hands & your brain simultaneously :)
  • 4. program your controller & your receiver
    A little bit of code
  • 5. (tips for) flight
    A lot of fun
  • 6. evaluation
    Don't forget that you must come and show your functional drone to the makers'lab manager before the end of explorations time!
