Customization of the Drone:


Change the design of the protections:




In Illustrator, resume the design of the protection We will use the "Pattern Shape Options" tool To do this, create a shape on the page next to the protection and select there Now, click on the box "Shape of the contour" at the top of your page it should contain by default a black line with mentioned "Basic" next

Click on the "New shape" icon and then in the "Pattern shape" dialog box The "Pattern Shape Options" dialog box appears. You can change the pitch and scale of the shape then press OK Select the black circle and always in the Contour Shape menu "select your previously created shape You can change the contour settings again in "Options of the selected object" Export your drawing in SVG and import it into Tinkercad as seen in Tutorial 2








Add decorations with laser cutting:

Shark : Created by Callum Taylor

Go to the Noun Project.com

Thanks to this site, you can very easily and freely download images in a vectorized format


Select an image on the site then click on "Download" and "SVG" then "Creative Commons"
(If asked, create a free account on the site)


Now, import your vectorized image into Illustrator.


As seen previously in the Makers' Toolbox To cut a laser shape, we need to create a red outline.
At first, we will display the layers panel using the "Window" menu and then "Layers", this will allow us to isolate the outline of the shape that we will create.
Then select the shape and go to the "Object"> "Path"> "Offset" menu.
Choose 0.1 mm and click on OK
Select the layer with the outline previously created, select without filling and then red outline with a thickness of 0.001mm. Hide the other layers with the eye button then with "Direct Selection Tool", erase the red lines inside the image.






To create a hat


Holding the left mouse button, select both cylinders.

Egg : Created by Eleanor Bell

The recommended maximum size for a hat is 40mm wide by 40mm length to not obstruct the propeller passages and a maximum weight of 5 grams. A heavy drone is easier to fly at the expense of flying time.
To create one of the screw holes in order to fix the cap, add two 2 mm by 2 mm red circles and set a distance of 24 mm between them. To do this, use the "Align" panel, the "Align on a key object" function and then the "Vertical distribution of space" button.
The file can now be saved to SVG or AI for export.
Feel free to combine different materials together like wood and Canson paper
